# 4A Market Research Report Template

Tailor this outline to the task. Keep sections that directly affect the user's decision and remove irrelevant sections.

- Brand/product/site.
- Business question.
- Target regions.
- Output requested.
- What this report is and is not.
- Researchable facts vs brand-side required facts.
